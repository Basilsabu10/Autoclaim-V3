Decision Matrix & Scoring

Claims are evaluated deterministically based on absolute rules and an aggregated severity score.

APPROVED

No failures, OR only LOW severity issues (score < 2).

Auto-approved processing.

MONITORED

Approved but with minor LOW severity flags (score 1).

Monitored post-processing.

FLAGGED

Score >= 2 but < 10.

Requires human review.

REJECTED

Any CRITICAL failure, OR score >= 10.

Auto-rejected for fraud/invalidity.

Severity Weights CRITICAL: 10, HIGH: 5, MED: 2, LOW: 1
Compounding Multiplier x1.5 (if >= 3 HIGH/CRITICAL fails)
